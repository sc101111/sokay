Black Star Wear
 Timati, Russia, Europe

TimeLine

  • Part I

    Black Star Wear brand presents clothes for the youngsters who are eager to express themselves, who are ambitious and think nothings about the opinion of others. The company Black Star Wear was established in 2010 by the popular singer Timati (music label Black Star inc.). Originally the brand was only a few T-shirts with prints made in limited edition but along with growing popularity of the brand, the decision was made to expand. Presently Black Star Wear brand develops independently, has three stores in Moscow and over 30 franchise stores in Russia. 

  • Part II

    Black Star Wear - russian street-wear brand, founded in 2010 as a part of Black Star.

    We are guided by the modern fashion and global trends. So, if you’re planning to create a truly stylish and fashionable wardrobe, you can find everything you need - from the basics, to unique garments with contrasting prints and cuts, that will make your look unique and memorable.

    Our product range includes women's, men's, teen’s and children's clothes. Also we release capsule collections of Black Star label’s artists like Timati, Mot, L'One, Kristina Si and others.

    The rhythm of modern life requires us to be ready for any situation, wherever we are. Therefore, your clothing should be both comfortable and stylish like Black Star Wear clothing.

    All products are made from high quality materials and have a comfortable fit. We prefer natural materials, eco-friendly, hypoallergic fabrics and we use 100% natural cotton in our blends for better fit.

    In 2015, we moved our production from China to Russia, and now it takes only 2 weeks for the garment to transform from the sketch to the clothing in the store.

    Black Star Wear online store has a function of pre-order from the new arrivals. Now you can be sure that you get our new clothes one of the first anywhere, because we deliver worldwide.

    Sign up for our e-mail newsletter to get special offers and latest Black Star Wear's news!

The brand offers capsule collection of singers under Black Star inc. label - Timati (Legion XIII), L`one (Cosmokot) and Hanna (GOLD X).
