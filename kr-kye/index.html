  • Part III

    The KYE brand is derived from the name of designer Han Hye, and made its debut in London Fashion Week in February 2011.
    KYE is a brand that strengthens fashion wit, humor and fun rather than seeking chic fashion that is fashion-conscious, and its main target audience is a customer who can afford to enjoy fashion and is aiming at foreign and domestic customers .
    Therefore, KYE's competitiveness is a combination of contemporary yet funky style and unique styling that can only be shown by designer Han Hee, who has not tried hard as a domestic designer.
    In particular, his work has earned a reputation for excellence in unleashing artistic motives into commercial products. Kai's Director Han Hye is a young graduate student and graduate student at Central Saint Martin University in London. She is involved in a variety of fashion fields including stylist, non-directed stylist, broadcast panel, and collaboration with overseas brands.
